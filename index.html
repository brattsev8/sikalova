Психолог Людмила Сикалова

Providing state-of-the-art psychological services for adults, youth, and children:

  • Consultation, comprehensive diagnosis and treatment planning
  • Psychotherapy ("talk" therapy for adults and adolescents. See: What is Psychotherapy?)
  • Play therapy (for young children)
  • Parent-child guidance and parent-infant therapy
  • Psychological evaluation (assessment for diagnosis, treatment, and academic planning)
  • Therapeutic mentorship (helping people who feel stuck or lost in career, life or relationships to find their way)
  • Clinical supervision

Ludmila Sikalova approach to treatment is integrative, culturally sensitive, and influenced by psychodynamic and developmental theory. She works with individuals, parents, and families.

Please call or email for a consultation. All hours are by appointment only, Monday - Friday. Go to Contact for inclement weather or holiday closing updates.
